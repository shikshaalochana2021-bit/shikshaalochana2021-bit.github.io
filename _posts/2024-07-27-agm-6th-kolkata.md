The **6th Annual General Meeting** of Shiksha Alochana was held in **Kolkata**, bringing together primary school teachers, academics, activists, and community members from across West Bengal.

The AGM is the primary annual forum where Shiksha Alochana reviews its work, shares learnings from across its network, and plans for the year ahead. Members travel from many districts to participate, making the AGM a significant gathering of people committed to improving primary education in West Bengal.

Before beginning any health interventions, Shiksha Alochana conducted a comprehensive baseline survey to understand the health awareness of students across participating schools.

The survey was designed in collaboration with the **Cardiological Society of India**, using a Google Form. More than **5,000 students** participated across the 150 schools involved in the programme.

The data gathered through this survey established a benchmark for measuring the impact of health education activities over the course of the programme. It revealed the starting level of knowledge about nutrition, hygiene, exercise, and preventable illness among primary school students in West Bengal.

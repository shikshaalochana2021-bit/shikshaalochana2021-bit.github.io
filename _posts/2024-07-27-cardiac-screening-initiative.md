As part of the School and Community Health Programme, Shiksha Alochana partnered with the **Cardiological Society of India** to conduct cardiac health screening for primary school students.

Echocardiography examinations were performed for **101 selected children** across five schools — four in North 24 Parganas and one in South 24 Parganas. Eminent cardiologists visited the schools and performed ECG assessments using a specialist mobile application with stethoscope support.

Future phases of this programme will expand the reach of cardiac screening and initiate treatment for children identified as needing care. This initiative represents a significant step in bringing specialist health services directly to children in government primary schools.

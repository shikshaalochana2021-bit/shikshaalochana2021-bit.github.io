Shiksha Alochana organised a special health awareness campaign across all 150 participating schools, working in clusters of five schools at a time.

The campaign brought together teachers, students, and parents to address key health themes through posters, assemblies, and community events. Topics included:

- **Food and nutrition** — healthy eating habits and balanced diets
- **Cleanliness and hygiene** — personal hygiene and school sanitation
- **Environment** — reducing plastic use and caring for the local environment
- **Exercise** — the importance of physical activity for children
- **Substance abuse** — awareness and prevention of drug and tobacco use

Each cluster developed their own campaign materials, with students taking an active role in creating posters and presenting to their communities. This approach built a sense of ownership and responsibility among students themselves.

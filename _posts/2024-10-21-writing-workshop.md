In October 2024, Shiksha Alochana organised a **Writing Workshop** (লেখার কর্মশালা) for primary school teachers and students across our network. The workshop focused on creative writing as a tool for learning — encouraging students to find their own voice and helping teachers create a more expressive classroom environment.

Participants explored different forms of writing: personal narratives, descriptive writing, poetry, and collaborative storytelling. Teachers also worked together to develop techniques for incorporating writing into everyday lessons across subjects.

Writing workshops like this are part of Shiksha Alochana's broader effort to make schools more joyful and expressive spaces. When children write, they are not just practising a skill — they are building a relationship with language, with ideas, and with each other.

We look forward to continuing these workshops across more schools in the coming year.

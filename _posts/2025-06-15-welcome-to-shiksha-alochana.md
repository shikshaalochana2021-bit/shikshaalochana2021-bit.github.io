We are delighted to welcome you to the new Shiksha Alochana website!

This site is the new home for our community of primary school teachers, academics, and activists working together to strengthen primary education across West Bengal.

## What You Will Find Here

- **About Us** — our history, objectives, and the values that guide our work
- **Activities** — details of our school health programmes, educational events, and community meetings
- **Resources** — teaching guides, reports, and materials for primary school educators
- **Wall Magazine** — our beloved community publication
- **News** — the latest updates, announcements, and reports from the Shiksha Alochana network

## Stay Connected

We will be regularly updating this site with news from our programmes, resources for teachers, and reports from events across West Bengal. We invite all teachers, parents, and education enthusiasts to explore the site and get in touch.

To reach us, visit our [Contact page](/contact/) or email us at [shikshaalochana2021@gmail.com](mailto:shikshaalochana2021@gmail.com).

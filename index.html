Shiksha Alochana — Discussions on Education

An initiative of primary school teachers, academics, and activists from West Bengal, working together to strengthen primary education through dialogue, collaboration, and shared purpose.

About Us Our Activities
Our Mission

What We Stand For

Shiksha Alochana brings together educators and community members to build a stronger foundation for primary education across West Bengal.

🌱

Strengthen Education

We work to enhance primary education in West Bengal by fostering professional development and sharing best teaching practices among teachers.

🤝

Build Community

We create spaces for primary school teachers, academics, and activists to connect, collaborate, and support one another across districts.

💬

Foster Dialogue

Through meetings, programmes, and publications, we promote open discussion about education between teachers and the broader public.

Shiksha Alochana is committed to sharing knowledge freely. This page brings together teaching guides, videos, reports, and other materials useful for primary school teachers in West Bengal.

---

## Teaching Guides

We develop and share practical teaching resources that primary school teachers can use directly in their classrooms.

*Teaching guides will be listed here as they are published. Please [contact us](/contact/) to request specific materials or contribute your own resources.*

---

## Videos

We share video content covering educational topics, classroom demonstrations, and recordings from our programmes and events.

*Videos will be embedded here. If you have footage from Shiksha Alochana events, please [get in touch](/contact/).*

## Reports & Documents

- **Activity Reports** — Summaries of our health programmes, school events, and community meetings.
- **Survey Findings** — Results from baseline health and learning surveys conducted in partner schools.
- **Annual Reports** — Year-in-review documents covering our work and impact.

*Documents will be available for download here. Please [contact us](/contact/) for copies of specific reports.*

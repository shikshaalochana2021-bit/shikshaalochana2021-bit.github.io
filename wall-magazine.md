The **Wall Magazine** (দেওয়াল পত্রিকা) is one of Shiksha Alochana's most cherished initiatives. A wall magazine is a traditional Bengali form of community publication — handwritten or printed and displayed publicly in schools — that gives everyone a voice: teachers, students, and community members alike.

---

## What Is a Wall Magazine?

A wall magazine is displayed on the walls of schools and community spaces, making it accessible to everyone. Unlike a formal publication, it is open, immediate, and deeply local. It might contain:

- Student writing — stories, poems, essays, artwork
- Teacher reflections on education and classroom life
- Reports on school events and community activities
- News from across the Shiksha Alochana network
- Discussions on important educational topics

---

## Our Wall Magazine

Shiksha Alochana's wall magazine is produced periodically and distributed to member schools. It is a space for teachers and students to express themselves, share their experiences, and contribute to the broader conversation about education in West Bengal.

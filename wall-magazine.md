At Shiksha Alochana, we believe that writing is not just a skill — it is a way of thinking, of seeing, and of belonging. Our **Story Writing** (গল্প লেখা) initiative creates space for primary school students and teachers to write, share, and celebrate their own stories.

Too often, children in government primary schools are never asked what they think or feel. Story writing changes that. When a child writes about their village, their family, their fears, or their joys, they are not just practising a language — they are asserting that their experience matters.

## Writing Workshops

Alongside the written collections, we run **writing workshops** (লেখার কর্মশালা) in schools — sessions where students write freely on themes of their choosing, with teachers as fellow writers rather than evaluators. These workshops have consistently shown that children have far more to say than most classroom settings allow.

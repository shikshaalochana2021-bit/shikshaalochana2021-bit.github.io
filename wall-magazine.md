The **Wall Magazine** (দেওয়াল পত্রিকা) is one of Shiksha Alochana's most cherished initiatives. A wall magazine is a traditional Bengali form of community publication — printed and displayed in schools — that gives everyone a voice: teachers, students, and community members alike.

Unlike a formal publication, it is open, immediate, and deeply local. Each issue contains student writing, poems, artwork, teacher reflections, and stories from across the Shiksha Alochana network.
